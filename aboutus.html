About us

The Restaurent

Welcome to MaazIndien Restaurent MaazIndien Restaurent opened its doors in the autumn of 2017 with one clear ambition: to serve authentic Indian food where our passion and traditions are reflected in every single dish, creating a truly unique dining experience. Since our humble beginnings, we have built strong relationships with our guests, always welcoming them with open arms and heartfelt enthusiasm. With every guest who has placed their trust in us, our passion has only grown. Thank you for being a part of our journey into the heart of India through food.

We promise to continue surprising and delighting you with authentic flavors that will leave you enchanted and full of joy. Welcome to MaazIndien Restaurent — where our love for food and tradition is transformed into unforgettable culinary experiences for all.
We serve delicious Indian dishes in a cozy atmosphere – and are known for our friendly staff and excellent service."
